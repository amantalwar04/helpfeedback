Duck weight

Domestic ducks fall into categories based on weight. These weight classes may help you choose a species based on how much you will need to feed your ducks.

Bantam
The lightest-weight ducks and best fliers, such as the Mallard duck.
Light
The second lightest-weight ducks and best egg-layers, such as the Khaki Campbell duck.
Medium
The mid-weight and generally calmest ducks, such as the Swedish duck.
Heavy
The heaviest and generally friendliest ducks, such as the Pekin duck.
