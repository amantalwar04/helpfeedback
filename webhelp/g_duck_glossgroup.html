Duck watching equipment

binoculars

An optical device for use with both eyes used to view distant objects

duck bait

Bait used to attract ducks. Can range from specialty pellets to cheap white bread

duck call

A device consisting of a tube with a reed contained inside it, used to imitate and attract ducks

spotting scope

An optical device for use with a single eye, which tends towards greater detail view than binoculars
